Call-for-Papers: JCertif Conference

The week-long JCertif Conference is one of the largest developer conferences in Africa. Share your development expertise and experience with the Java community and tech professionals in Africa by submitting a proposal for the 2014 conference agenda.

JCertif will take place in Brazzaville, Congo on September 8-14. The deadline for papers is August 15th.
