Project News

NetBeans 6.0 Now Available in Beta!

NetBeans.org is proud to announce the availability of NetBeans IDE 6.0 Beta. Download it now and see why it's the only IDE you need. The focus of NetBeans IDE 6.0 is improved developer productivity through a smarter, faster editor, and the integration of all NetBeans products into one IDE. Highlights of the NetBeans IDE 6.0 release include:

* Swing GUI Builder
* Intelligent Editor
* Ruby on Rails Support
* Visual JSF Design
* Web Services & SOA
* XML Schema Editor, XSLT Designer
And more.

The final NetBeans IDE 6.0 release is planned for late 2007. As always, we welcome and encourage your feedback about your experience using the NetBeans IDE on our mailing lists and in your blogs!

NetBeans IDE Wins BOSSIE Award!

InfoWorld has awarded the NetBeans IDE a BOSSIE award for Best Enterprise IDE. BOSSIE stands for the Best of Open-Source Software. The InfoWorld staff concluded: "For its commitment to quality over quantity and for the courage to rip and replace modules to improve developer productivity, we salute the NetBeans team and its stellar IDE."

Consuming StrikeIron Web Services in Web Applications

NetBeans 6.0 Beta includes new functionality that allows users to consume partner web services from the IDE. These services can be consumed from web applications. StrikeIron, a NetBeans partner, hosts web services that solve business problems. This tutorial shows how to use these web services from StrikeIron to build a Visual Web application.
