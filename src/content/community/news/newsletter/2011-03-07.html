Issue # 477 - Mar 07, 2011

Project News

NetBeans Governance Board Elections - Nominations Open!

It's time to elect new members for the 18th cycle of the NetBeans Governance Board! Participate in the process by nominating NetBeans community members that you think should be considered for the Board. You can even nominate yourself!

The DEADLINE for nominations is Monday, March 21 at midnight in the last time zone.

GlassFish 3.1 Available for Download

The newly released GlassFish Server Open Source Edition 3.1 offers full clustering capabilities, centralized admin and a host of other features. Check out the GlassFish team blog for links to documentation, articles, demos and more.

New Release of the NetBeans APIGen Plugin

A new build of the PHP APIGen support for the NetBeans IDE plugin (v1.0.3) is available for download.
